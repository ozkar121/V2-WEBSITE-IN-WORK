## Why Travel by Helicopter in Mexico City?

Mexico City spans over 1,400 km² with a metropolitan population exceeding 21 million people. Traffic is a chronic problem affecting both residents and business travelers. A helicopter completely transforms the equation:

**Time.** A transfer from Toluca Airport to Santa Fe or Polanco takes 15 minutes by helicopter versus 45 to 90 minutes by ground (depending on traffic and time of day).

**Productivity.** Every hour stuck in traffic is an hour lost from work, meetings, or rest. The helicopter gives that time back.

**Security.** Air transfers eliminate exposure to ground traffic conditions and offer a controlled door-to-door environment.

**Guaranteed punctuality.** There are no traffic jams at 500 meters altitude. The helicopter arrives when it needs to arrive.
